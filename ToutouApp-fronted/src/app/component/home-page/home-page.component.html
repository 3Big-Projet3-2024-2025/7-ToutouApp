How Does It Work?

1. Submit a request for dog care

Describe your needs for dog care and create a request asking for help.

2. Wait for someone to accept your request

Neighbors interested in your request will respond and agree to take care of your dog.

3. Communicate with your helper

Discuss the details with the person to ensure everything is well-organized for your dog's care.
